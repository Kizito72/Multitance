About Multitance International Limited

Building bridges across continents through trusted trade partnerships

Our Story

Founded in 2013, Multitance International Limited has grown from a regional trading company to a globally recognized partner in international commerce. Headquartered in the vibrant business district of Victoria Island, Lagos, we have built a reputation for excellence, integrity, and innovation in cross-border trade.

Our strategic location in Nigeria, Africa's largest economy, positions us uniquely to facilitate trade between African markets and the rest of the world. With a branch office in Owerri and partnerships spanning five continents, we bring local expertise with a global perspective.

Our Mission

To facilitate seamless international trade by providing reliable, efficient, and compliant import-export solutions that drive mutual growth for our partners across the globe.

Our Vision

To become Africa's most trusted international trade partner, recognized globally for excellence in service delivery, innovation, and sustainable business practices.
